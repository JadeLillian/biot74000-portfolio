Hello — I'm **Jade Mulholland**, a student in the Bachelor of Applied Biotechnology
(Honours) program at Conestoga College.

I have spent 3 years working in the food safety industry while completeing my degree. I have worked in food maufacturing at Agropur Dairy Cooperative, research at Conestoga's Food Research and Food Innovation Lab, quality control at Ferrero and quality assurance at Capsule factory Neutraceuticals. My program at Conestoga emphasises hands on lab skills in microbiology and analytical chemistry instrumentation. I am currently completing a capstone research project which analyses soil bacteria biodiversity. 

Bioinformatics is important to the food industry becasue it gives us the tools and knowledge to present large amounts of data clearly. In the food industry, researchers gather data on the pH, water activity, moisture content and microbiological load of food products. This data needs to be visualized to understand food safety trends over time. 

This portfolio documents the bioinformatics tools, methods, and skills I have
developed, and how I have applied them.
